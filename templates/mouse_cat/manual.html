How do I play?

In this game, two players face each other in a complex real-time strategy game.

One will be playing the mouse, whose objective is to get to the other side of the board, while the other one will be the cats and attempt to block him so he can't move.

While there are four cats and only one mouse, the mouse player has an ace up his sleeve: he can move both forwards and backwards, while his opponents the cats can only move forward.

Joining a game

To begin, you need to join an existing game, or create your own. To do so, click on the "Play" button in the menu above. There, you can see all the games you're participting on, as well as join new games.

If no games show up in the New games section, do not fear: with a click on the "New game" button you will have created your own, and as soon as someone joins it you will be set to play. Bear in mind you can only have one open empty game (in which no one has joined yet) at the same time.

Controlling your pieces

If it's your turn, you can click any of your pieces to see the possible moves, which will be highlighted. After selecting one piece, you can then click in one of the possible squares to perform a move. Then, the turn switches to your opponent, and you must wait until he makes a move.
